About Me

I am originally from Spain and traveled to the United States at the age of 14 to pursue my education. I consider myself to be a creative and open-minded person, with a passion to continue my journey in self-development.

I enjoy creating a product that pairs minimalism and user-friendliness together seamlessly. When designing, I take on the identity of my user to better understand my design and refine the product with my knowledge of UX/UI design.

As someone heavily involved in self-improvement, I’ve decided to minor in psychology in order to further educate myself on the psychology behind everyday thought processes. This assists me to tailor the most suitable product for my user’s needs. My goal is to work with a like-minded company who shares my ambition to improve the world.
